ABOUT THE PROJECT

IDETATION & PROBLEM

USER RESEARCH

The goal of the project was to address the “paper problem” that the CCC was experiencing. The CCC lacked an electronic corporate travel system, all travel and associated processes were paper based. As a result, a web and mobile app solution was formulated to solve the problem. In the team, I lead the user experience strategy for the project, including user research, user analysis and user interface design. The solution has placed 3rd in Algonquin College's Applied Research Day event where 91 teams compete in Algonquin College.
