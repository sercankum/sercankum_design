RESTASSURED MEDICAL APPLICATION

ROLE: UI/UX DESIGNER

About The Project

Approximately 10 to 15 per cent of Canadian children and adults suffer from eczema. This means that up to 5.4 million Canadian suffer from this disease The image recognition market is estimated to grow from USD 15.95 Billion in 2016 to USD 38.92 Billion by 2021.This solution only gives suggestions and never give medical advice.

My Role as UI/UX Designer

  • Facilitated Ideation and Value proposition creation
  • Conducted User Research and personas
  • Sketched out interaction specs, and user flows
  • Wireframesm, high-fi mockups, low-fi to high-fi prototypes
  • UI Design
  • Usability Testing

    • The Challenge and Ideation

    Patients lack the tools and the assurance that their treatment is effective with respect to their skin condition. Eczema may reappear at any time, especially during times of stress. This could mean that the treatment given by the doctor may not be working.

    User Research

    Wanted to find out who our users were and how they would use this tool. Conducted in Person interviews with users who have eczema. In person survey was conducted and 20 people responed.

    Sketching & Wireframes

    Sketched out ideas of potential solutions on paper and iterated 3 times.Usability tested the sketches after every iteration.

    Mobile Prototype

    Created a high fidelity prototype in Adobe XD and added colour.Usability tested the high fidelity prototype and found out some errors such as lack of understanding of percentages and making the export feature easier.

    Solution

    The solution was to create a Mobile Application where patients can take pictures of their skin condition and get valid feedback regarding their progress.
